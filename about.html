About

So..a little about me. After I graduated college, I dabbled in a variety of professions, from English teaching to Accounting. While I enjoyed both professions in the time that I did get a chance to explore them, I ultimately felt that English teaching was a bit too unstable for my liking, whereas Accounting lacked the creativity I needed to keep me coming back for more. Eventually, I was drawn to web development because I felt like it was an amalgamation of everything I was looking for! Finally, I found a profession that offered high future growth potential and an outlet for me to explore my creative side. Now that I have found my dream profession, I plan on pursuing this endeavor with passion and vigor. When I'm not improving my coding skills or learning more about the tech scene, you'll find me working on my handles at the local YMCA, playing videogames, or cuddling with my super cute baby nephew Ethan. :)
