About

So..a little about me. After I graduated college, I dabbled in a variety of professions, from English teaching to Accounting. While I enjoyed both professions in the time that I did get a chance to explore them, I ultimately felt that English teaching was a bit too unstable for my liking, whereas Accounting lacked the creativity I needed to keep me coming back for more. Eventually, I was drawn to web development because I felt like it was an amalgamation of everything I was looking for! As I was messing around with development, I was also exploring the field of cryptocurrency, and when I learned that the world of crypto also needs developers, I was even more ecstatic. Having recently completed Stephen Grider's course "Ethereum and Solidity: The Complete Developer's Guide," and with a couple projects under my belt, I am hoping to be able to do some real-world programming in this avant-garde field.
